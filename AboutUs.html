About The Keystone Series

The Keystone Series Esports League is dedicated to addressing critical challenges in the esports industry, including a lack of development pathways, infrastructure, and centralized player statistics.

Our Mission

We aim to unify local scenes nationwide, standardize competition, use player statistics to improve skills, and connect players with recruiters. Our goal is to create a seamless ecosystem and a talent pipeline through a unified platform.

Our Solution

  • Unclear Pathway: We help players who want to grow their career but don't know where to start.
  • Lack of Infrastructure: We provide a central platform for player statistics and talent showcasing.
  • Seamless Ecosystem: We connect players with recruiters and use player statistics to help improve skills.

Market Potential

Our esports league has significant growth potential with an estimated market size of $1.6 billion by 2029. In the US alone, there are approximately 15 million potential users within the FGC (Fighting Game Community) and over 8,500 high schools that can benefit from our platform.

Business Model

  • Sponsors and Ad Spaces: Revenue from high schools, colleges, and pro teams.
  • Subscription: Offering advanced analytics to players to enhance their gameplay.
  • Data: Using data analytics for player improvement and matchmaking.
